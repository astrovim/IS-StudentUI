Online Registration Application
1. Go to Registration.
2. Fill up the form, make sure that is true and specific.
3. Click "submit" after filling up the form.

Certificate of Eligibility
Provide Certificate of Eligibility to study in the Philippines from Department of Education

Authenticated copy of Good Moral Certificate (from previous school)
Scholastic Records/Transfer of Credentials

Submit Scholastic Records/Transfer of Credentials duly authenticated by the Philippine Consulate General or Philippine Embassy with English Translation, if written in other foreign language ("SEEN and NOTED" stamp is not acceptable)
Birth Certificate of Applicant

(1) If born abroad with foreign citizenship - Birth Certificate duly authenticated by the Philippine Consulate General or Philippine Embassy,
(2) If born abroad with Filipino Parent/s - Philippine Statistics Authority Report of Birth, (3) If born in the Philippines with Filipino Parent/s - Birth certificate duly authenticated by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA)
2"x2" Identical ID Pictures

Six (6) copies of 2"x2" Identical ID Pictures white background with printed name and signature of applicant at the back.
For INC Members:
Certificate of Church Membership from Locale Congregation. (Patotoo ng Lokal)

For Non-INC Members:
Letter of Intent from the applicant.
Endorsement Letter from the INC member and a Local Resident Minister of INC Locale nearest to applicant's residence attesting to his/her being good and law abiding citizen.
